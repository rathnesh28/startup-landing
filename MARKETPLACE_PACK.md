## Full Description

LaunchPad is built for founders, indie hackers, and agencies who want a polished SaaS landing page without starting from scratch.

This template includes a modern hero, feature cards, premium pricing layout, trust-focused about section, validated contact form, final conversion CTA, and a clean modern footer.

It is fully responsive, easy to customize, and production-build ready with Vite.
